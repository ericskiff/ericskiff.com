Hi, I’m Eric Skiff, and I’m one of the founders of Tanooki Labs, where we’re working at the forefront of implemented AI. We build web & mobile apps, agents that power automated workflows, custom ML models for large brands, RAG systems, deep research pipelines, and push the cutting edge of AI-enabled development.

I’m also a cofounder of Reportable News, and previously a hacker at the innovation lab QLabs and a Drop.io alum.

Community

I love building communities as much as I love building software. I organize VibeCamp, a one-day unconference all about building with AI in NYC. We hosted our first one last year and are doing another this year. Come build with us.

I’m a cofounder of NYC Resistor, the Brooklyn hackerspace where makers, hackers, and artists come together to learn and create. I’ve also organized several BarCamp events across NYC, helping connect and grow the NY tech scene.


About Me:
